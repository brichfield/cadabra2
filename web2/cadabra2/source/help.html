Manual pages

Every algorithm and every property has a manual page with examples, which you can view online:

Algorithms

CcanonicaliseBring a tensorial expression to canonical form by re-ordering indices.
collect_factorsCollect identical factors in a product.
collect_termsCollect identical terms in a sum.
completeComplete a set of substitution rules to cover related objects.
Ddecompose_productDecompose a product of tensors by using Young projectors.
distributeRewrite a product of sums as a sum of products.
Eeliminate_kroneckerEliminate Kronecker delta symbols by performing index contractions.
evaluateEvaluate components of a tensorial expression.
expand_diracbarRewrite a Dirac bar of a composite spinor expression in simpler form.
Fflatten_sumFlatten an expression containing nested sums.
Jjoin_gammaJoin the product of two generalised gamma matrices into a sum of single ones.
Kkeep_termsKeep terms which satisfy a constraint on their weights.
Llr_tensorMake a Littlewood-Richardson product of two Young tableaux.
OorderReorder objects, if necessary with sign changes.
Pproduct_ruleApply the product rule to a derivative acting on a product.
Rrename_dummiesRename dummy indices.

Forum

All questions about using Cadabra can be posted on the Cadabra Q&A forum. The forum accepts LaTeX formulas and is fully searchable.

The old mailing list has been closed as it does not offer as much functionality as the new forum.
